Apple Music Turns 10

Written by: Maddie

  • They launched a new global hub for artists! Designed to foster creativity and connection.
  • A new playlist for users tailored to their favorite songs from the past 10 years. To no ones surprise, Taylor Swift was the vast majority of my playlist.
  • They are also revealing the top 500 most-streamed songs.
  • They made a block of specials and live programming for this week.
